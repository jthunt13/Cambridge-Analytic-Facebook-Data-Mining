
NOV. 26, 2017

Cyndia Susan Clegg and Karl Anton Maier were married Nov. 24 by Mayor Tobias Gantert of Ühlingen-Birkendorf, in the German state of Baden-Württemberg. The marriage took place in the Rathaus in the village of Berau, which is part of the municipality of Ühlingen-Birkendorf.
The bride, 70, is the distinguished professor of English at Pepperdine University, in Malibu, Calif. She graduated from the University of California, Los Angeles, from which she also received both a master's degree and a Ph.D. in English.
She is the author of “Shakespeare's Reading Audiences” (Cambridge University Press, 2017).
Dr. Clegg is a daughter of Virginia M. Clegg of Tujunga, Calif., and the late Wayne D. Clegg. The bride’s father retired as a television news photographer for KTTV in Los Angeles. Her mother retired as a real estate agent there.
The groom, 72, retired as a management supervisor at On Ideas, an advertising firm in Jacksonville, Fla. He graduated from Wesleyan University and received an M.B.A. from Columbia University.

He is the son of the late Ann Wagner Maier and the late Alfred A. Maier, who lived in Port Washington, N.Y. The groom’s father retired as an electrical engineer at the P.R.D. Corporation in Syosset, N.Y., a wholly owned defense systems subsidiary of the Harris Corporation.
The bride, whose first marriage ended in divorce, was a widow. The groom’s previous marriage ended in divorce.

The couple met in October 2015 at All Saints Episcopal Church in Pasadena, Calif., when Mr. Maier asked Dr. Clegg if he could share her hymnal.
